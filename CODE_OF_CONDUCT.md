# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want ConvexKit to be a welcoming, practical place for people building with TanStack Start, Convex, Better Auth, and Cloudflare.

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or technology preferences.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, patient, and direct.
- Assume good intent, but make room for correction.
- Keep feedback focused on the work and its impact.
- Prefer clear examples, reproduction steps, and actionable suggestions.
- Respect maintainers' time and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting comments, or repeated disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that would reasonably make the project unsafe or hostile

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants whose behavior violates this code of conduct.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the repository maintainer with relevant context and links. Reports will be handled as privately and fairly as possible.
